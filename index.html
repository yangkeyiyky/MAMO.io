带着孩子认识世界

Introducing the world to children

当下越来越多的学者探讨如何训练AI、如何“喂养”高质量的数据集，但却少有人探讨AI对人的“反哺”。
如果有一天人与机器的角色互换由AI来教育（训练）人类，它真的是客观且理性的吗？它又是否会在教育孩子的同时携带偏见？
At present, more and more scholars are discussing how to train AI and how to "feed" high-quality data sets, but few people discuss the "feedback" of AI to people. If one day the roles of humans and machines are exchanged and AI is used to educate (train) humans, is it really objective and rational? Does it carry prejudice while educating children?
